Agriculture Supply Chain using Blockchain Technology

During my diploma, I worked with two teammates on a project to build an Agriculture Supply Chain system using blockchain technology. We created a smart contract that allows all participants in the supply chain to enter their data directly into the blockchain. This way, the information is securely stored and shared with everyone involved, making it transparent, unchangeable, and distributed across the network. The project helped me understand how blockchain can bring trust and clarity to complex systems like agriculture supply chains.

AI/ML-Powered Health Insurance Premium Prediction Model & LLM-Based Policy Assistant Chatbot

This was a team project where we set out to build a health insurance premium prediction system using machine learning. Our main goal was to make the process more personalized and fair by predicting premiums based on a person's health profile, lifestyle, and other important factors, rather than using a one-size-fits-all approach. This way, insurers could better assess individual risk and even offer tailored preventive measures. To make the system easier for users to interact with, we also implemented a chatbot powered by a large language model (LLM). The chatbot helps answer policy-related questions and guides users in a friendly, conversational way.
